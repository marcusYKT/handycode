Celebrating 40 years of TIFF®

To celebrate 40 years of TIFF® I designed the branding and user experience for a video booth located in the TIFF Bell Lightbox. Users are asked to record their favourite TIFF® memory as well as congratulate TIFF® on 40 years.

You can check out the booth from now until the end of fall at the TIFF Bell Lightbox.
